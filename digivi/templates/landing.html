Project Overview

Transforming Agriculture Through Technology

DIGI-VI represents a paradigm shift in agricultural technology, leveraging advanced IoT sensors, real-time data analytics, and machine learning algorithms to optimize water usage and crop yields. Our platform integrates seamlessly with existing farm infrastructure while providing actionable insights that empower farmers to make data-driven decisions. Through the TIET-TAU Center of Excellence for Food Security (T2CEFS), we're addressing critical challenges in sustainable agriculture and climate resilience, aligned with UN Sustainable Development Goals 2030.
